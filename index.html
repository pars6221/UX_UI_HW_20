Take Off Mobile App Case Study

A travel app that will give the user a safe peace of mind, offering low travel deals with flight cancellation and delay insurance policies with redeemable bonuses and perks for the traveler.

I was part of a team of talented individuals that worked together to see this project from the beginning of a simple idea to the creation of a clickable prototype. My duties were, but no limited to, creative personnel, UX Designer and Researcher, as well as Interface Designer.

Research

We conducted User Interviews and researched competitors to get a better understanding of the wants and needs of the modern day traveler. We took this information and expanded our ideas for what we needed to be included in our travel app.

Definition and Ideation

From our research we were able to create a user Persona for our app. From this we had gotten a better understanding and better insight for the overall consistency for our travel app.

Prototype and Final Thoughts

With all of our information and competitor analysis, our team went forth to put together wire frames and a usable prototype. We took the time and effort to make sure the layout was clear and easy to maneuver for the user.

With more time and development, our team would like to look deeper into perks and disocunts this this app wuld be abe to offer.
